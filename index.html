The Studio

We design spaces that feel personal, not Pinterest-copied.


HouseThat is a young boutique architecture and interior design studio creating bespoke homes with a bold, minimal and detail-driven approach. Our work is guided by individuality, lifestyle, spatial rhythm and storytelling.

Design Philosophy

Every home deserves its own voice. Instead of repeating trends, we craft spaces that feel connected to the people living in them.

Not Template-Based

Every project starts from the client’s lifestyle, not from a fixed formula.

Bold Minimalism

Clean layouts, strong typography, premium textures and confident details.

Portfolio First

Work, details and experience speak louder than generic company content.

Visual Direction
